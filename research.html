Research & Validation

Evidence-based development advancing quality education through rigorous testing

Study Design

GANIT was developed as a home-based audiovisual app, built on Android App Studio for traditional smartphones. The modules utilize language-free symbols and emojis with interactive audio to increase accessibility, ensuring children from diverse backgrounds can benefit from the tool.

Clinical Foundation

Our app is built on DSM-5 criteria, ensuring it meets the highest standards for therapeutic intervention for children with math anxiety and dyscalculia. This clinical foundation provides a solid base for addressing the specific needs of neurodiverse learners.
