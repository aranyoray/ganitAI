Improving Access to Quality Education

Quality education should be accessible to every child, regardless of their learning differences or socioeconomic background. Currently, no therapeutic tools exist for children with math anxiety and/or dyscalculia, creating a significant barrier to quality education. There is an urgent pedagogical need to improve math skills in alignment with NEP 2020 and ensure that all children have equal opportunities to succeed.

Enhancing Teaching and Learning Experiences

GANIT introduces a Generative AI-driven math learning app that gamifies concept uptake, improves math confidence, and reduces errors. Our home-based audiovisual app utilizes language-free symbols, emojis, and interactive audio to increase accessibility for all children, making quality education a reality for underserved communities.

Promoting Lifelong Learning

By building confidence and foundational numeracy skills early, we're creating a pathway for lifelong learning. Children who overcome math anxiety develop a positive relationship with learning that extends far beyond the classroom, setting them up for success throughout their educational journey and beyond.
